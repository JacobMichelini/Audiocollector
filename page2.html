Dilemn - Shock

Dilemn - Funk Your Soul

Aha! You only though I liked relaxing music? Nope. This is Dilemn, a french guy who primarily does electro-house, although he meanders into dubstep and mid-tempo. Amazing energy and total dancefloor material with a great classic bit of french filtered sound. This music is just fucking fun. Although not heard here, he also has a penchant for the occasional odd melodic choice, which may grate to some people but is actually kind of refreshing in my opinion. A little tension never killed anyone.

ProleteR - Back Home

ProleteR - Soul Key

Really entertaining hip-hop beats constructed with a big swing and jazz influence. Some really great piano and sax samples and even some turntablism mixed in. What more could you want? This stuff is gauranteed to put you in a great mood.

Teddy Killerz - Bellagio

Don't be put off by the silly name and cover art, the music is great and I'd actually describe most of this album as "classy". Basically great drum and bass and slower tempo stuff with some really really funky sampled basslines. The whole album is very solid, and cohesive. Good DnB is hard to find and he's one of my favorites right now.

Phaeleh - Journey

Phaeleh - A Different Time

On a different note, Phaeleh generally produces incredibly clean beautiful mostly-dubstep tracks with impeccable production values. Some of his output can be a bit boring to me, but others are perfect minimal masterpieces. So clean.
