Au5 & Fractal - Smoke

Prismatic - Minty (Au5 & Fractal Remix)

Wild crazy fast-cut electro and dubstep has gotten a bit old lately. The wierd noises and effects were fresh and cool when not everyone and their brother knew how to make them. Now I can find approximately one million bedroom produces making that type of track. However, Au5 and Fractal do manage to produce some music a considerable step above the crowd. Basically they just do everything at an expert level. Au5's mastering as also insanely good (and so fucking loud), perhaps the best around for this type of music. These track have nice recognizable themes which make them more memorable than the average electrostep blah.

Phonat - Ride The Prejudice

Phonat - All This Time

Pure glitchy pretty music. Who knows what genres this fits under, but it's impeccable and interesting with all sorts of cool percussion and nice melodies as well. This album was also released on Skrillex's label OWSLA. He has very interesting taste. I find this is really good stuff to work or study while listening to.

Joris Voorn - Ringo

This is a very good house track. I actually really love to listen to a really steady and relaxing progressive or deep house track. Its so refreshing just to hear the play on a single theme, and a house beat can really put you in a nice groove. Unfortunately house tracks that aren't just flat out boring are about as rare as diamond unicorns. This is one of those unicorns.

Akamatsu - Océan Générique

Akamatsu - Je Dérive

Again I don't really know how to classify this. Tiny sounding little soft beeping music with cute little mouse scratching percussion. Its just really nice. Maybe sort of similar to the music C148 did for minecraft, but more active and concrete. The name is japanese but the artist is french. Hopefully he releases some more music...just one album.
