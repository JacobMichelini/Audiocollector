ChrisB. - Fluid Dynamics

ChrisB. - Eye In the Sky

This is somewhere in the glitch-hop/dubstep area. Sounds a bit like earlier Glitch Mob mixed with Mimosa with a bit more of a psychadelic bent and more strings. Great mastering and solid melodic backing for each track. Actually pretty laid back stuff but has some more aggressive sounds.

ODESZA - How Did I Get Here

ODESZA - If There's Time

Sample based gorgeous mostly instrumental hip-hop. Super warm and fuzzy record crackle type sounds, although they sometimes stray into just poorly mastered territory. Mega-catchy to the extreme. If these guys picked up a vocalist they would be huge, but hey I like it this way. Great summer relaxation music.

Akara - Sky Dancer

Akara - Evensong

To tell the truth I'm not really sure who these guys/girls are, but they clearly have access to a talented singer and an assortment of other musicians. This stuff is just beautiful semi-orchestral psychadelic electronica. Hell I don't even know what language this is, but its fucking great. The posted tracks are more straightforward, but they also go in some stranger directions.

Tor - Loop Theory

Tor - Aperture

Speaking of chill sample based instrumental hip-hop. Perhaps similar to Bonobo/Emancipator/Aether. I really like this because each track has a totally memorable melodic character to it. Sometimes mellower stuff can end up blending into a big unrecognizable cloud of nice noises. This music completely avoids that.
