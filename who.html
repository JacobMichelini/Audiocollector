Who is this guy?

Thats me! I made this site!

I'm Jacob Michelini, devoted electronic music enthusiast and web/UX designer currently living in San Francisco CA. Over the years I have acquired a computer filled not only with hundreds of unfinished songs starts I've created...but also much better full length ones I've collected from artists from every nook and corner of the internet. I've made this blog to help show that there is an incredible range of amazing electronic sound out there beyond banging kick drums that put you to sleep.

If anyone finds any artists or tracks here and is having trouble tracking down the files, just let me know and I may be able to give you a hand and some more recommendations. Support great musicians though! Most of these guys and girls are struggling to make a living!

Let me know if you have any questions or requests: Devoq81@gmail.com
